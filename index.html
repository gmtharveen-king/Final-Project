Global Competition

The 2026 FIFA World Cup will see 48 nations battle it out across three nations. Teams will be divided into 16 groups of 3, creating unprecedented competition and opportunities for every squad to make their mark on the world stage.

From emerging powerhouses to traditional champions, every team brings their unique style, passion, and determination to win the most prestigious trophy in football.

Tournament Timeline

Group Stage

June 12 - July 5, 2026

48 teams compete in 80 matches across all three countries. Every match is crucial as teams battle for advancement.

Round of 16

July 6 - 10, 2026

The intensity ramps up as 16 teams remain. Knockout matches decide who advances to the quarterfinals.

Quarterfinals

July 11 - 13, 2026

Eight teams remain. Dreams continue or end on the pitch as quarter-final matches determine the elite four.

Final Tournament

July 14 - 19, 2026

Semifinals and the grand finale. Two teams battle for football immortality in the championship match.
